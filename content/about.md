I'm Finn Rutis. I work on systems and developer tooling, and this blog is where I write down things I learn along the way.

Most posts here will cover technical notes, small projects, and practical problems I run into — often around Nix, infrastructure, and building software. I write them partly to remember what I figured out, and partly in case someone else finds the answer useful.

Posts are written as I learn, so expect rough edges, corrections, and updates over time.
